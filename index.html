Privacy Policy

OzSurfAlarm — Last updated: May 2026

Overview

Short version: OzSurfAlarm does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All your settings stay on your device.

OzSurfAlarm is a surf condition alarm app for Android. This policy explains what data the app uses and how it is handled.

Data We Collect

OzSurfAlarm does not collect any personal data. There are no user accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, and no backend server operated by us.

Your alarm settings (surf location, swell thresholds, alarm time, etc.) are stored locally on your device only using Android's standard storage. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

OzSurfAlarm fetches surf forecast data from two external services. When the app makes these requests, your device's IP address is visible to these services as a standard part of how the internet works. We do not control what these services log.

Service Purpose Privacy Policy
Bureau of Meteorology (BOM) Live wind observations bom.gov.au
Open-Meteo Swell and wind forecasts open-meteo.com

Permissions

OzSurfAlarm requests the following Android permissions:

Permission Reason
POST_NOTIFICATIONS To send you the surf alarm notification
SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM To fire the alarm at your chosen time
USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENT To display the alarm as a full-screen alert

None of these permissions are used to collect data about you.

Children's Privacy

OzSurfAlarm does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
